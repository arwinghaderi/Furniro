Payment Options

We offer a variety of payment options to suit your needs:

  • Credit Card: Pay with your Visa, MasterCard, or American Express. Simply enter your card details at checkout.
  • PayPal: Use your PayPal account for a secure and fast payment experience. Select PayPal at checkout and follow the instructions.
  • Cash on Delivery: Pay with cash when your order is delivered. Available in select areas only.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I get a refund?

A: Refunds can be processed through the original payment method. Please contact our support team for assistance.

Q: Is my payment information secure?

A: Yes, we use advanced encryption technology to protect your payment information.
